Hostel Rules and Regulations

  1. Boarders should read the rules and regulations before signing the application form (A copy of rules is available at the office).
  2. Boarders must turn off all the electrical equipment & lights before leaving their rooms.
  3. Boarders are not allowed to use electric stoves, heaters, etc., in rooms.
  4. Boarders are not allowed to organize any group activities in their room.
  5. Food will be served only in the designated dining halls and only during the specified timings. Not dining in a hostel may call for discontinuation.
  6. Late comers is strictly NO until prior approval by Warden
  7. Boarder must keep their respective Rooms clean. Defacing walls, equipment, furniture etc., is strictly prohibited.
  8. Birthday/Other Celebrations are strictly prohibited in Hostel.
